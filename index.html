Mission

What to expect

Our mission at PRCC is to embrace the divine calling to be the light of the Word (Mt 5:14-15) and the salt of the earth (Mattew 5:13). We are committed to fulfilling our responsibilities with unwavering dedication, guided by clear qualifications and a code of conduct for all ministry workers. Beyond mere obedience, we strive to bear lasting fruits, cultivating an enduring impact in alignment with God's eternal plan.

Vision

At Power of Resurrection Christian Church (PRCC), our vision is to reach the celestial goal of making heaven at the end of this earthly race. We aspire not only for personal salvation but to extend the invitation to as many souls as possible, ensuring a heavenly gathering that transcends this temporal existence.
